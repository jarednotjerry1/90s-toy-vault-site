The G.I. Joe collecting market operates on a different level. We're not talking about figures worth $50 or even $500. We're talking five and six figures for the right piece in the right condition.

Here are the most valuable G.I. Joe items based on verified auction sales:

**$200,001: Original 1964 Prototype**
Comic dealer Steve Geppi paid this at Heritage Auctions in 2003 for the prototype from Don Levine's personal collection. The most expensive action figure ever sold.

**$50,000+ (estimated): Rubiplas Cobra White Mortal**
A South American exclusive so rare that a beater with visible wear goes for $5,000. A gem mint example is estimated at $50,000 or more. Mint on card? Easily six figures.

**$26,400: 1982 Snake Eyes (Straight-Arm, AFA 85 NM+)**
Sold at Heritage in 2021. The most expensive 3.75-inch figure at auction.

**$5,000+: USS Flagg (Complete, Boxed)**
Seven feet of accidental production history in a box.
